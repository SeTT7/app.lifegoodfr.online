For iPhone & iPad

Use Safari to add the app to your home screen.

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
  4. Done! Find the angel icon on your home screen and open it like any app.
Note: This only works on Safari (the blue compass icon). Chrome on iPhone doesn't support installation.

For Android

Install in one tap with Chrome.

Loading installer...

  1. Tap the menu (⋮) in the top-right of Chrome.
  2. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app".
  3. Confirm — the angel icon appears on your home screen.
